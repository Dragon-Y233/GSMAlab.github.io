**This team is the gas-sensitive materials and algorithm research team of the High Voltage Group of the School of Electrical Engineering of Guangxi University. It is mainly engaged in the theoretical and applied research of machine olfactory technology, involving the entire link from materials, devices, algorithms to systems.**  
我们是广西大学电气工程学院高压组的气敏材料与算法研究团队。主要从事机器嗅觉技术的理论与应用研究，涉及从材料、器件、算法到系统的全环节。
